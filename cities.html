    CITIES

    Some of my favourite cities are mentioned below

    LAHORE

    Pakistan's Lahore is the country's second-largest city and is in the Punjab province. The city is frequently referred to as Pakistan's cultural capital because of its extensive cultural heritage. The Badshahi Mosque and the Lahore Fort are two examples of some of the most magnificent Mughal- era structures in Lahore. The city is well-known for its mouthwatering food, especially its spicy street food, which draws foodies from all over the world. Lahore is a must-visit destination for anyone interested in experiencing Pakistan's cultural and historical richness because of its bustling bazaar culture.

    KARACHI

    Karachi, Pakistan's largest city, can be found on the country's southern coast with a view of the Arabian Sea. People from all over Pakistan and other nations live and work in the city, which is known for its multicultural and diverse population. Clifton Beach and Manora Island, two of Pakistan's most stunning beaches, can be found in Karachi, home to some of Pakistan's most popular tourist attractions. The city is a foodie's dream because it has a thriving food scene with options from around the world as well as traditional Pakistani fare. In spite of confronting difficulties connected with framework and security, Karachi stays an energetic and dynamic city that draws in guests from around the world because of its rich social legacy and financial significance.

    ISLAMABAD

    Pakistan's capital, Islamabad, can be found at the foothills of the Margalla Hills in the north of the country. The city was planned during the 1960s by a Greek draftsman and is known for its advanced design and very much arranged format. Islamabad is a center of government movement and home to numerous significant government structures, including the Parliament House and the High Court of Pakistan. The city is likewise a significant place for global strategy, with numerous international safe havens and offices situated there. Islamabad is known for its lovely parks, including the Margalla Slopes Public Park and the Fatima Jinnah Park, which give a serene break from the city's buzzing about.
